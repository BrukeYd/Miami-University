Four Cornerstones for the future

Our financial goal for this campaign - $1 billion - reflects the magnitude of our aspirations. We believe that by strategically investing in four campaign Cornerstones -Scholarships, Business and Enterpreneurship, Clinical Health Sciences, and Digital Innovaation and Technology - we can address critical global concerns and positively impact the university for decades to come.

Scholarships

GOAL: $450,000,000

A Miami education shouldn't depend on economic circumstances. Our campaign target of $450 million for need-based scholarships will help ensure access and affordability for qualified students, regardless of circumstances. In addtion, our University Honors College, Prodesse Scholars program, and Presidential Fellows program will put a Miami degree in reach for more young scholars.

Business and Enterpreneurship

GOAL: $300,000,000

Miami University has long fostered an ecosystem of enterpreneurship. We have made great strides in cultivating leaders and idea-generators throug campus-wide initiatives that nurture collaboration and innovative thinking. With this proof of concept in hand, the time has come for a substantial investment in this ecosystem, marking us as a national model of business education for socail good, and establishing a center for venture funding, a startup accelerator, and much more.

Clinical Health Sciences

GOAL: $150,000,000

A new vision to unify and coordinate healthcare and science education across the University offeres a bold opportunity to shape our impact in the clinical health Sciences. The field of physician assistants is predicted to grow by one-third by 2026, and we plan to meet that need with innovative, ethical, best-in-class graduates. New programs, infrastructure, and talent all contribute to this transformative potential as Miami leads forward in the science and practice of healthcare.

Digital Innovation and Technology

GOAL: $100,000,000

The world's data doubles every two years. At this staggering pace, it's no longer enough to keep current - we must anticipate the growth, speed, and direction of the digital universe. A transdiscipliary program in data science and digital innovation will bring together multiple departments, while a new data science building will serve as a launch pad for research, dialogue, and a deeper understanding of how the digital universe continually shapes our world. This isn't simply a new program, it is a reinvention of what education can be - and what it will become.
